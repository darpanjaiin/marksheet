Why i built this

Namastey, I'm CA Darpan Jain, a CA who codes for fun, and this web app is my way of giving back to students like you. A couple of years ago, I scratched "95 in costing" above my desk. It wasn't just a number; it was a promise. I didn't hit 95, but I scored higher than the All India Rank 1 of my attempt in that subject, a win I never saw coming.

I've watched juniors do it too. One stuck her target on the fridge; another made his goal his phone lockscreen. Today, they're both CAs. Small dreams don't wear CA badges. That's why I built this tool in an hour: to help you see your CA self now. No ads, no sign-ups, just a free space to craft your marksheet and own your ambition.

This isn't about tech for me. It's about belief. I know the grind, late nights, self-doubt, the weight of those exams. If pinning your marks here lifts even a bit of that, I've done my job. You're built for more than passing; you're built for greatness. Make it real, one mark at a time.

If this sparked something for you, I'd love to hear your story.
